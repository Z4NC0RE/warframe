Factions

There are five factions so far that you have to fight against.

Gineer:

The Grineer are the first enemies you will face in the game, lead as a violet military to take over the Origin System.

Originally Lead by Captain Vor the one responsible for your awakening wanted to use your power to aid him in taking over.

You fight back against Captain Vor and eventually kill him, However Vor was holding The Janus Key a very powerful item, this granted him immortality with the cost of being stuck in the void.
