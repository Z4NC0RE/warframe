What is Warframe?

Waframe is a third person shooter game that lets you a Tenno an ancient race of warriors who has awoken with no memories of the past. The tenno have and ability to take control of a Warframe and use their abilities to help in the fight against the Gineer, Corpus, Infested, Orokin, and Sentients.

Each warfame has four abilites to use in the fight each taking different amounts of energy and vary in what they can do

Warframe also have stats that you can use mods in increase each stat to make them stronger.
